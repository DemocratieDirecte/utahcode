34A-3-104.   Employer liability for compensation.
(1)  Every employer is liable for the payment of disability and medical
benefits to every employee who becomes disabled, or death benefits to the
dependents of any employee who dies, by reason of an occupational disease
under the terms of this chapter.
(2)  Compensation shall not be paid when the last day of injurious exposure of
the employee to the hazards of the occupational disease occurred prior to
1941.
