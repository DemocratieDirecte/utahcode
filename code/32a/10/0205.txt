32A-10-205.   Bond.
(1)  Each on-premise beer retailer licensee shall post a cash or corporate
surety bond in the penal sum of $2,000 payable to the department, which the
licensee has procured and must maintain for so long as the licensee continues
to operate as an on-premise beer retailer licensee.
(2)  The bond shall be in a form approved by the attorney general, conditioned
upon the licensee's faithful compliance with this title and the rules of the
commission.
(3) (a)  If the $2,000 surety bond is canceled due to the licensee's
negligence, a $300 reinstatement fee may be assessed.
(b)  No part of any cash or corporate bond so posted may be withdrawn during
the period the license is in effect, or while revocation proceedings are
pending against the licensee.
(c)  A bond filed by a licensee may be forfeited if the license is finally
revoked.
