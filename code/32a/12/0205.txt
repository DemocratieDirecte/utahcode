32A-12-205 (Repealed 07/01/11).   Unlawful sale or supply to interdicted
persons.
(1)  A person may not sell, offer to sell, or otherwise furnish or supply any
alcoholic beverage or product to any known interdicted person.
(2)  This section does not apply to the furnishing or supplying of an
alcoholic beverage or product to an interdicted person upon the prescription
of a physician, or administered by a physician, dentist, or hospital under
this title.
