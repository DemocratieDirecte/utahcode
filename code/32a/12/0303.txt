32A-12-303.   Tampering with records.
(1)  Any official or employee of the commission or the department who has
custody of any writing or record required to be filed or deposited with the
commission or the department under this title, and who steals, falsifies,
alters, willfully destroys, mutilates, defaces, removes, or conceals in whole
or in part that writing or record, or who knowingly permits any other person
to do so, is guilty of a third degree felony.
(2)  Any person not an official or employee of the commission or the
department who commits any of the acts specified in Subsection (1) is guilty
of a class B misdemeanor.
