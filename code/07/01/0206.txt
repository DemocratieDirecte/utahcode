7-1-206.   Supervisor of savings and loan associations -- Responsibilities.
(1)  The commissioner shall designate an examiner as supervisor of savings and
loan associations who shall be sufficiently qualified by training and
experience in the savings and loan business or as an employee of a state or
federal savings and loan supervisory agency to perform the duties of the
office.
(2)  The supervisor of savings and loan associations is responsible, subject
to the direction and control of the commissioner, for the general supervision
and examination of all savings and loan associations and related institutions
subject to the jurisdiction of the department. The supervisor shall assist and
advise the commissioner in the execution of the laws of this state relating to
these institutions, and shall perform other duties prescribed in this title or
assigned by the commissioner.
