58-31b-601.   Minimum standards for nursing programs -- Medication aide
training.
(1)  A nursing education program shall be affiliated with an accredited
institution of higher education in order to be approved by the division.
(2)  The minimum standards a nursing program shall meet to qualify graduates
for licensure under this chapter shall be defined by division rule.
(3)  In accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 3, Utah Administrative Rulemaking
Act, and the provisions of this chapter, the division shall make rules
defining the minimum standards for a medication aide certified training
program to qualify a person for certification under this chapter as a
medication aide certified.
