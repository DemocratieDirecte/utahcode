58-1-302.   License by endorsement.
The division may issue a license without examination to a person who has been
licensed in any state, district, or territory of the United States or in any
foreign country, whose education, experience, and examination requirements
are, or were at the time the license was issued, equal to those of this state.
Before any person may be issued a license under this section, he shall produce
satisfactory evidence of his qualifications, identity, and good standing in
his occupation or profession.
