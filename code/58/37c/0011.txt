58-37c-11.   Penalty for unlawful conduct.
(1)  Any person who violates the unlawful conduct provision defined in
Subsections 58-37c-3(12)(a) through (j) is guilty of a class A misdemeanor.
(2)  Any person who violates the unlawful conduct provisions defined in
Subsection 58-37c-3(12)(k) is guilty of a second degree felony.
