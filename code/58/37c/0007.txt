58-37c-7.   License classifications.
(1)  The division shall issue to persons qualified under the provisions of
this chapter and rules adopted a license in the classifications:
(a)  controlled substance precursor distributor; or
(b)  controlled substance precursor purchaser.
(2)  It is unlawful for a person to engage in the distribution, sale,
transfer, or in the purchase or obtaining of a controlled substance precursor
in a regulated transaction without being licensed or excepted from licensure
under this chapter.
