58-60-402.   Definitions.

In addition to the definitions in Sections 58-1-102 and 58-60-102, as used in this part:

(1)  "Board" means the Professional Counselor Licensing Board created in Section 58-60-403.

(2)  "Practice of professional counseling" means the practice of mental health therapy by means of observation, description, evaluation, interpretation, intervention, and treatment to effect modification of human behavior by the application of generally recognized professional counseling principles, methods, and procedures for the purpose of preventing, treating, or eliminating mental or emotional illness or dysfunction, symptoms of any of these, or maladaptive behavior.
