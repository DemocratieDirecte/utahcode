58-39a-5.   Qualifications for certification.
Applicants for certification as an alternative dispute resolution provider
shall:
(1)  submit an application in a form as prescribed by the division;
(2)  pay a fee as determined by the department under Section 63J-1-504;
(3)  be of good moral character; and
(4)  complete a program of education or training, or both, in ADR or have
demonstrated sufficient experience in ADR, as determined by the division in
collaboration with the board.
