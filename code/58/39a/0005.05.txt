58-39a-5.5.   Term of certificate -- Expiration -- Renewal.
(1)  Each certificate issued under this chapter shall be issued in accordance
with a two-year renewal cycle established by rule.  A renewal period may be
extended or shortened by as much as one year to maintain established renewal
cycles or to change an established renewal cycle.
(2)  Each certificate automatically expires on the expiration date shown on
the certificate unless renewed by the certified ADR provider in accordance
with Section 58-1-308.
