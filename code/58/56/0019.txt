58-56-19.   Standardized building permit numbering.

(1)  As used in this section, "project" means a "construction project" as defined in Section 38-1-27.

(2)  The division shall develop a standardized building permit numbering system for use by any compliance agency in the state that issues a permit for construction.

(3) (a)  Beginning on July 1, 2009, a compliance agency issuing a permit for construction shall use the standardized building permit numbering system described under Subsection (2).

(b)  A compliance agency may not use a numbering system other than the system described under Subsection (2) to define a building permit number.

(4)  The standardized building permit numbering system described under Subsection (2) shall include a combination of alpha or numeric characters arranged in a format acceptable to the compliance agency.
