58-40-7.   Recreational therapy treatment or intervention plans.
(1)  Under this chapter, a recreational therapy treatment or intervention plan
shall be maintained for each patient.
(2)  The plan shall include:
(a)  an assessment based on the strengths, limitations, interests, and needs
of the patient;
(b)  prescribed intervention strategies and approaches;
(c)  patient goals and  measurable treatment or intervention objectives and
outcomes; and
(d)  evaluations of progress and treatment.
