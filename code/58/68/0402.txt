58-68-402.   Authority to assess penalties.
(1)  After acting in accordance with Title 63G, Chapter 4, Administrative
Procedures Act, and Title 58, Chapter 1, Division of Occupational and
Professional Licensing Act, the division may impose administrative penalties
of up to $10,000 for each act of unprofessional conduct under this chapter.
(2)  Assessment of a penalty under this section does not affect any other
action the division is authorized to take regarding a license issued under
this chapter.
