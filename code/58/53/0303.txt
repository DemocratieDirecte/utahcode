58-53-303.   Term of license -- Expiration -- Renewal.

(1) The division shall issue each license  under this chapter in accordance with a two-year renewal cycle established by rule. The division may by renewal extend or shorten a  license expiration date by as much as one year to stagger the renewal cycles it administers.

(2)  Each license automatically expires on the expiration date shown on the license unless renewed by the licensee in accordance with Section 58-1-308.
