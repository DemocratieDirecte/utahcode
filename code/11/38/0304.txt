11-38-304.   Commission to report annually.
The commission shall submit an annual report to the Executive Appropriations
Committee of the Legislature:
(1)  specifying the amount of each disbursement from the program;
(2)  identifying the recipient of each disbursement and describing the project
for which money was disbursed; and
(3)  detailing the conditions, if any, placed by the commission on
disbursements from the program.
