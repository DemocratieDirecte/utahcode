57-8a-202.   Unpaid assessment -- Costs and attorney fees.
(1)  An association may recover all expenses incurred by the association in
collecting an unpaid assessment, including reasonable attorney fees.
(2) (a)  An association may maintain an action to recover a money judgment for
an unpaid assessment without foreclosing or waiving the lien securing the
unpaid assessment.
(b)  The prevailing party in an action described in Subsection (2)(a) may
recover:
(i)  costs; and
(ii)  reasonable attorney fees.
