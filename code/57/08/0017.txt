57-8-17.   Records of receipts and expenditures -- Availability for
examination.
The manager or management committee shall keep detailed, accurate records in
chronological order, of the receipts and expenditures affecting the common
areas and facilities, specifying and itemizing the maintenance and repair
expenses of the common areas and facilities and any other expenses incurred.
Records and the vouchers authorizing the payments involved shall be available
for examination by the unit owners at convenient hours of weekdays.
