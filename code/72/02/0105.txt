72-2-105.   Budgetary accounts within Transportation Fund -- Disposition of
unexpended balances.
(1)  The amount designated by the Legislature, out of which the items budgeted
shall be paid, shall be established in appropriation and allotment accounts
within the Transportation Fund.
(2)  At the close of the biennium all unexpended balances remaining in the
accounts so budgeted shall be closed to the fund balance account of the
Transportation Fund.
