26-21-4.   Per diem of committee members.
(1)  Members shall receive no compensation or benefits for their services, but
may receive per diem and expenses incurred in the performance of the
member’s official duties at the rates established by the Division of Finance
under Sections 63A-3-106 and 63A-3-107.
(2)  Members may decline to receive per diem and expenses for their service.
