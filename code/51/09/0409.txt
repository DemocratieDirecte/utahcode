51-9-409.   Guardian Ad Litem Services Account established -- Funding --
Uses.
(1)  There is created in the General Fund a restricted account known as the
Guardian Ad Litem Services Account, for the purpose of funding the Office of
Guardian Ad Litem, in accordance with the provisions of Sections 78A-6-901 and
78A-6-902.
(2)  Except as provided in Subsection (3), the Division of Finance shall
allocate 1.75% of the collected surcharge established in Section 51-9-401 to
the Guardian Ad Litem Services Account.
(3)  The amount allocated under Subsection (2) may not exceed the amount
appropriated to the Guardian Ad Litem Services Account by the Legislature.
