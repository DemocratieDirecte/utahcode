30-1-37.   Confidentiality of information obtained under counseling
provisions.
Except for the information required or to be required on the marriage license
application form, any information given by a marriage license applicant in
compliance with this act shall be confidential information and shall not be
released by any person, board, commission or other entity. However, the
premarital counseling board or board of commissioners may use the information,
without identification of individuals, to compile and release statistical
data.
