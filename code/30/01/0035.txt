30-1-35.   Persons performing counseling services designated by board --
Exemption from license requirements.
For the purposes of this act the premarital counseling board of each county or
combination of counties may determine those persons who are to perform any
services under this act and any person so acting shall not be subject to
prosecution or other sanctions for his failure to hold any license for these
services as may be required by the laws of the state of Utah.
