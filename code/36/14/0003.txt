36-14-3.   Contents.

Each legislative subpoena shall include:

(1)  the name of the legislative body or office on whose behalf the subpoena is issued;

(2)  the signature of the issuer;

(3)  a command to the person or entity to whom the subpoena is addressed to:

(a)  appear and testify at the time and place set forth in the subpoena;

(b)  appear and testify before some legislative body or office and produce accounts, books, papers, documents, or other tangible things designated in the subpoena; or

(c)  produce accounts, books, papers, documents, or other tangible things designated in the subpoena at the time and place designated in the subpoena.



Enacted by Chapter 174, 1989 General Session
