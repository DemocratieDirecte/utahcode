36-1-102.   Election of senators -- Staggered terms.
(1)  Unless otherwise provided by law, each senator elected from Senate
Districts 1, 6, 8, 10, 13, 14, 16, 19, 20, 23, 24, 25, 27, and 29 at the 2000
General Election shall serve out the term of office for which he or she was
elected and shall represent the realigned district if he or she resides in
that district.
(2)  At the general election to be held in 2002, senators elected from Senate
Districts 2, 3, 4, 5, 7, 9, 11, 12, 15, 17, 18, 21, 22, 26, and 28 shall be
elected to serve a term of office of four years.
