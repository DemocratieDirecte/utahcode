48-2c-607.   Notice of series -- Articles of organization.
(1)  Notice in a company's articles of organization of the limitation on
liabilities of a series, as required by Section 48-2c-606, is sufficient
whether or not the company has established any series at the time the notice
is included in the articles of organization.
(2)  The notice required by Section 48-2c-606 need not reference any specific
series.
(3)  The filing of the notice required by Section 48-2c-606 with the division
constitutes notice of the limitation on liability of a series.
