78B-3-418.   Decision and recommendations of panel -- No judicial or other
review.
The panel shall render its opinion in writing not later than 30 days after the
end of the proceedings.  The panel shall determine on the basis of the
evidence whether each claim against each health care provider has merit or has
no merit and, if meritorious, whether the conduct complained of resulted in
harm to the claimant.
There is no judicial or other review or appeal of the panel's decision or
recommendations.
