53-6-304.   Waiver of training course requirement.
(1)  The director may waive the required basic dispatcher training course and
certify an applicant who:
(a)  provides evidence that the applicant meets the requirements under Section
53-6-302, relating to qualifications for admission to the training course;
(b)  provides evidence that the applicant has completed a basic dispatcher
training program that, in the director's judgment, is equivalent to the course
required for certification under this part; and
(c)  passes a written examination, an oral examination, or both, that affirms
the applicant's ability in public safety communications.
(2)  An applicant who fails the examination under Subsection (1)(c) shall
complete the basic dispatcher training course and pass the dispatcher
certification examination to become certified.
