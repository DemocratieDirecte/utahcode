53-6-102.   Definitions.
As used in this chapter:
(1)  "Certified academy" means a peace officer training institution certified
in accordance with the standards developed under Section 53-6-105.
(2)  "Council" means the Peace Officer Standards and Training Council created
in Section 53-6-106.
(3)  "Director" means the director of the Peace Officer Standards and Training
Division appointed under Section 53-6-104.
(4)  "Dispatcher" means an employee of a public safety agency of the state or
any of its political subdivisions and whose primary duties are to:
(a) (i)  receive calls for one or a combination of, emergency police, fire,
and medical services, and to dispatch the appropriate personnel and equipment
in response to the calls; and
(ii)  make urgent decisions affecting the life, health, and welfare of the
public and public safety employees; or
(b)  supervise dispatchers or direct a dispatch communication center.
(5)  "Division" means the Peace Officer Standards and Training Division
created in Section 53-6-103.
(6)  "POST" means the division.
