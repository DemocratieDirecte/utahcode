53-5-708.   Permit -- Names private.
(1)  When any permit is issued, a record shall be maintained in the office of
the licensing authority.  Notwithstanding the requirements of Subsection
63G-2-301(2)(b), the names, addresses, telephone numbers, dates of birth, and
Social Security numbers of persons receiving permits are protected records
under Subsection 63G-2-305(10).
(2)  Copies of each permit issued shall be filed immediately by the licensing
authority with the division.
