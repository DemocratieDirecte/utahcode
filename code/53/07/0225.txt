53-7-225.   Times for sale and discharge of fireworks.

Class C common state approved explosives may be:

(1)  sold:

(a)  after June 19 and before July 26;

(b)  after December 19 and before January 3; and

(c)  15 days before and on the Chinese New Year;

(2)  discharged three days before, on the day of, and three days following:

(a)  July 4;

(b)  July 24;

(c)  January 1; and

(d)  the Chinese New Year.
