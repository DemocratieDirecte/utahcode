53-7-209.   Inspection of buildings by officials.
(1)  A fire chief or officer may enter any building or premises not used as a
private dwelling at any reasonable hour to inspect the building or premises
and enforce the rules made under this part, including the state fire code
adopted under Section 58-56-4.
(2)  The owner, lessee, manager, or operator of any building or premises not
used as a private dwelling shall permit inspections under this section.
