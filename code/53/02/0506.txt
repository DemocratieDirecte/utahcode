53-2-506.   Duties of participating political subdivisions.

Each participating political subdivision shall:

(1)  identify potential hazards that could affect the participating political subdivision;

(2)  conduct joint planning, intelligence sharing, and threat assessment development with contiguous participating political subdivisions and conduct joint training with them at least biennially;

(3)  identify and inventory the services, equipment, supplies, personnel, and other resources related to participating political subdivision's planning, prevention, mitigation, response, and recovery activities; and

(4)  adopt and implement the standardized incident management system approved by the division.
