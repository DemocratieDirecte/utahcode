63G-6-205 (Superseded 05/01/13).   Delegation of authority.

Subject to rules and regulations, the chief procurement officer may delegate authority to designees or to any department, agency, or official.  For a procurement process under Title 63M, Chapter 1, Part 26, Government Procurement Private Proposal Program, any delegation by the chief procurement officer under this section shall be made to the Governor's Office of Economic Development.
