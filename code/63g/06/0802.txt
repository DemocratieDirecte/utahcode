63G-6-802.   Effect of timely protest.
In the event of a timely protest under Subsection 63G-6-801(1), 63G-6-810(1),
or 63G-6-815(1), the state shall not proceed further with the solicitation or
with the award of the contract until all administrative and judicial remedies
have been exhausted or until the chief procurement officer, after consultation
with the head of the using agency or the head of a purchasing agency, makes a
written determination that the award of the contract without delay is
necessary to protect substantial interests of the state.
