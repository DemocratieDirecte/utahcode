63G-6-102.   Purpose of chapter.
The underlying purposes and policies of this chapter are:
(1)  to simplify, clarify, and modernize the law governing procurement by this
state;
(2)  to ensure the fair and equitable treatment of all persons who deal with
the procurement system of this state;
(3)  to provide increased economy in state procurement activities; and
(4)  to foster effective broad-based competition within the free enterprise
system.
