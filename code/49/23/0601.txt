49-23-601.   Long-term disability coverage.
Each participating employer shall cover the following employees under Title
49, Chapter 21, Public Employees' Long-Term Disability Act, or a substantially
similar long-term disability program:
(1)  public safety employees initially entering employment on or after July 1,
2011;
(2)  firefighter service employees initially entering employment on or after
July 1, 2011; and
(3)  volunteer firefighters.
