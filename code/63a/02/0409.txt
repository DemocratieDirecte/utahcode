63A-2-409.   Disposal of certain surplus property by the legislative branch.

This part does not apply to disposal by the legislative branch of surplus property that is information technology equipment or a personal handheld device, if the Legislative Management Committee, by rule, establishes its own policy for disposal, by the legislative branch, of surplus property that is information technology equipment or a personal handheld device.



Enacted by Chapter 427, 2012 General Session
