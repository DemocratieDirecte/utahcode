63A-1-109.5.   Department authority to operate a division as an internal
service fund agency -- Exception.
(1)  Except as provided in Subsection (2) and subject to Section 63A-1-114 and
provisions governing internal service funds or internal service fund agencies
under Title 63J, Chapter 1, Budgetary Procedures Act, the department may
operate a division described in Section 63A-1-109 as an internal service fund
agency.
(2)  The department may not operate the division described in Subsection
63A-1-109(1)(f) as an internal service fund agency.
