17-27a-104.   Stricter requirements.
(1)  Except as provided in Subsection (2), a county may enact an ordinance
imposing stricter requirements or higher standards than are required by this
chapter.
(2)  A county may not impose stricter requirements or higher standards than
are required by:
(a)  Section 17-27a-305;
(b)  Section 17-27a-513;
(c)  Section 17-27a-515; and
(d)  Section 17-27a-519.
