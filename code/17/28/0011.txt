17-28-11.   Temporary work -- Term or period.
The head of any county fire department coming within the provisions of this
act may with the advice and consent of the county legislative body, appoint to
any position or place of employment in his fire department, any person for
temporary work without making such appointment from the certified civil
service list, provided, however, such appointment shall not be longer than one
month in the aggregate in the same calendar year.
