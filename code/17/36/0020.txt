17-36-20.   Purchases or encumbrances by purchasing agent.
All purchases or all encumbrances on behalf of any county shall be made or
incurred only upon an order or approval of the person duly authorized to act
as purchasing agent for the county, except encumbrances or expenditures
directly investigated and reported by the county auditor and approved by the
governing body. Unless otherwise provided by the governing body, the budget
officer or such officer's agents shall serve as purchasing agent.
