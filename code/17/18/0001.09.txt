17-18-1.9.   Creation of prosecution district by ordinance or interlocal
agreement.
(1)  The county governing body may create a countywide state prosecution
district by ordinance.
(2) (a)  Two or more counties, whether or not contiguous, may unite to create
and maintain a state prosecution district by interlocal agreement pursuant to
Title 11, Chapter 13.
(b)  At the time of the creation of the prosecution district, the
participating counties shall be located within the same judicial district.
(3)  The county governing body or bodies shall not dissolve a prosecution
district during the term of office of an elected or appointed district
attorney.
