31A-30-211.   Insurer disclosure.

(1)  The Health Insurance Exchange shall provide an employer and an employer's producer with the group's risk factor used to calculate the employer group's premium at the time of:

(a)  the initial offering of a health benefit plan; and

(b)  the renewal of a health benefit plan.

(2)  For health benefit plans that renew on or after March 1, 2012:

(a)  a carrier in the small employer market under Part 1, Individual and Small Employer Group, shall provide an employer and the employer's producer with premium renewal rates at least 60 days prior to the group's renewal date; and

(b)  the Health Insurance Exchange shall provide an employer who is participating in the defined contribution arrangement market of the Health Insurance Exchange and the employer's producer with premium renewal rates at least 60 days prior to a group's renewal.
