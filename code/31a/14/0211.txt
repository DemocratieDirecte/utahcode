31A-14-211.   Restrictions on foreign title insurers.

(1)  An authorized foreign title insurer may not insure property in this state except:

(a)  through a title insurance producer who is a resident in Utah;

(b)  through a bona fide branch office in Utah under the direction and control of the title insurer that pays all the expenses of the branch office including compensation of all employees; or

(c)  through a subsidiary title insurer authorized to do business in Utah.

(2)  This section does not apply to reinsurance.
