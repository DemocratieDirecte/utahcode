31A-35-607.   Filing of forms -- Department maintains files.

(1)  In accordance with Section 31A-21-201, each bail bond surety shall file with the commissioner a sample copy of each form the bail bond surety uses in the bail bond surety's bail bond surety business.

(2) A form described in Subsection (1) shall be filed:

(a)  before the form is first used by the bail bond surety; and

(b)  if the form is changed after it is filed under Subsection (2)(a).

(3) (a)  The department shall maintain and make available for public inspection a file regarding each bail bond surety.

(b)  The forms required to be filed under this section shall be maintained in the submitting bail bond surety's file.
