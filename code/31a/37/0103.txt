31A-37-103.   Chapter exclusivity.
(1)  Except as provided in Subsection (2) or otherwise provided in this
chapter, a provision of this title other than this chapter does not apply to a
captive insurance company.
(2)  In addition to this chapter, and subject to Section 31A-37a-103:
(a)  Chapter 37a, Special Purpose Financial Captive Insurance Company Act,
applies to a special purpose financial captive insurance company; and
(b)  for purposes of a special purpose financial captive insurance company, a
reference in this chapter to "this chapter" includes a reference to Chapter
37a.
