31A-23a-115.5.   Use of customer service representative.

A customer service representative licensed under this chapter:

(1)  may not maintain an office independent of the customer service representative's licensed producer or consultant employer for the purpose of conducting insurance activities;

(2)  except as provided in Subsection (3), may not sell, solicit, negotiate, or bind coverage; and

(3)  may provide a customer a quote on behalf of the customer service representative's licensed producer or consultant employer.
