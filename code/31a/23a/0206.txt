31A-23a-206.   Special requirements for variable contracts line of authority.

(1)  Before applying for a variable contracts line of authority, a producer or consultant shall be licensed under Section 61-1-3 as a:

(a)  broker-dealer; or

(b)  agent.

(2) A producer's or consultant's variable contracts line of authority is revoked on the day the producer's or consultant's license under Section 61-1-3 is no longer valid.
