31A-28-118.   Stay of proceedings -- Reopening default judgments.
All proceedings in which the insolvent insurer is a party in any court in this
state shall be stayed 60 days from the date an order of liquidation,
rehabilitation, or conservation is final to permit proper legal action by the
association on any matters germane to its powers or duties.  The association
may apply to have a judgment under any decision, order, verdict, or finding
based on default set aside by the same court that made the judgment.  The
association shall be permitted to defend against the suit on the merits.
