4-25-12.   Permitting swine to trespass after notice -- Class "C"
misdemeanor.
The owner or person in control of any swine that permits it to trespass upon
the improved private property of another person within three days after
receipt of written notice demanding that the owner or person in control
prevent further trespass is guilty of a class "C" misdemeanor; provided, that
the owner or person in control of the swine is entitled to a reasonable time
after notice for removal of the trespassing animal.
