4-11-5.   County bee inspector -- Appointment -- Termination --
Compensation.
The county executive upon the petition of five or more persons who raise bees
within their respective counties shall, with the approval of the commissioner,
appoint a qualified person to act as a bee inspector within their county. A
county bee inspector shall be employed at the pleasure of the county executive
and the commissioner, and is subject to termination of employment, with or
without cause, at the instance of either. Compensation for the county bee
inspector shall be fixed by the county legislative body.
