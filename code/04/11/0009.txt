4-11-9.   Inspection of apiaries where queen bees raised for sale -- Honey
from apiaries where queen bees raised for sale not to be used for candy for
mailing cages unless boiled.
(1)  At least twice each summer the county bee inspector shall inspect each
apiary in which queen bees are raised for sale.  No person shall sell or
transport any queen bee from an apiary which is found to be infected with
disease.
(2)  No person engaged in raising queen bees for sale shall use any honey for
making candy for mailing cages which has not been boiled for at least 30
minutes.
