4-11-6.   Hives to have removable frames -- Consent of county bee inspector
to sell or transport diseased bees.
(1) No person shall house or keep bees in a hive unless it is equipped with
movable frames to all its parts so that access to the hive can be had without
difficulty.
(2)  No person who owns or has possession of bees (whether queens or workers)
with knowledge that they are infected with disease, or with knowledge that
they have been exposed to disease, shall sell, barter, give away, or move such
bees, colonies, or appliances without the consent of the county bee inspector.
