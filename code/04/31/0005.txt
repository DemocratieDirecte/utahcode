4-31-5.   Imported dairy cattle -- Tuberculosis certificates.
No person shall bring cattle into this state for dairy or breeding purposes,
unless they are certified as having been examined and tested negative for
tuberculosis within 30 days before shipment to Utah. The certificate required
by this section may be waived if the commissioner determines the cattle are
shipped directly to Utah from an area approved by the United States Department
of Agriculture as an accredited tuberculosis free area.
