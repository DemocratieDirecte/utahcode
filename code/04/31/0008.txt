4-31-8.   Tuberculosis and Bangs Account created -- Exclusive use of revenue.

There is created within the General Fund a restricted fund account known as the "Tuberculosis and Bangs Disease Control Account." Revenue deposited in the Tuberculosis and Bangs Disease Control Account shall be used exclusively by the commissioner for the payment of expenses in administering tubercular tests and Bangs disease tests and for the payment of indemnities for slaughtered tubercular and Bang reacting dairy cattle.
