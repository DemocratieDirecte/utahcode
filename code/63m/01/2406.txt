63M-1-2406.    Report to the Legislature.

The office shall report annually to the Legislature's Workforce Services and Community and Economic Development Interim Committee and the Utah Tax Review Commission describing:

(1)  its success in attracting new commercial projects to development zones under this part and the corresponding increase in new incremental jobs;

(2)  the estimated amount of tax credit commitments made by the office and the period of time over which tax credits will be paid; and

(3)  the economic impact on the state related to generating new state revenues and providing tax credits under this part.
