63M-1-1505.   Criteria for participation -- Report.
(1)  The advisory board shall develop objective criteria including the
following:
(a)  a three year commitment by the applicant to provide a project manager
with a travel and operating budget;
(b)  evidence that both the business community and the local government
support the Utah Pioneer Communities Program approach philosophically and
financially;
(c)  capacity for economic change as a result of being a participant in the
program;
(d)  geographic location, population, and economic base diversity;
(e)  evidence of past preservation efforts; and
(f)  a population of less than 50,000.
(2)  The advisory board shall provide to the governor and to the presiding
officer of each house of the Legislature an annual report on the effects of
the Utah Pioneer Communities Program.
