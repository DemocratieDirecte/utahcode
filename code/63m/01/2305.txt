63M-1-2305.   Annual report.
(1)  The office shall make a report to the Legislature's Workforce Services
and Community and Economic Development Interim Committee by October 1 of each
year until the restricted account is terminated under Subsection
63M-1-2303(5)(b).
(2)  The report required by Subsection (1) shall provide information
concerning deployment of broadband service using grants from the restricted
account, pending applications, the balance remaining in the restricted
account, and suggested appropriations to the restricted account to achieve the
purposes of this part.
