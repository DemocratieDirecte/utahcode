19-9-115.   Tax exemption of property, income, and obligations of authority.
All property acquired or held by the authority is declared to be public
property used for essential public and governmental purposes.  All property
owned by the authority and all income derived from it is exempt from taxation
by the state or its political subdivisions.  All principal and income derived
by holders of obligations issued by the authority is also exempt from taxation
by the state or its political subdivisions, except for the corporate franchise
tax.
