19-6-715.   Recycling fee collection procedures.

(1)  The lubricating oil vendor shall pay the fee collected under Section 19-6-714 to the commission on or before the last day of the month following the calendar quarter in which the sale occurs.

(2)  The lubricating oil vendor may retain a maximum of 2% of the recycling fee it collects under Section 19-6-714 for the costs of collecting the fee.

(3)  The payment of the fee to the commission shall be accompanied by a form provided by the commission.
