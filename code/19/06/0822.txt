19-6-822.   Criminal penalties.
A knowing violation of any applicable provision of this part is a third degree
felony, except that any violation:
(1)  involving hazardous waste is governed by Title 19, Chapter 6, Part 1,
Solid and Hazardous Waste Act;
(2)  of Subsection 19-6-804(1) or (3) is subject only to the civil penalties
in Subsection 19-6-804(4); and
(3)  of Subsection 19-6-808(1), (2), or (3) is subject to the penalties under
Subsection 19-6-808(4).
