19-6-606.   Enforcement.
(1)  The board may authorize inspections under Section 19-6-104 of any place,
building, or premise where lead acid batteries are sold to determine
compliance with this part.  The board may authorize inspections under this
subsection only as funding is available within the department's current
budget.
(2)  Local health departments established under Title 26A may enforce the
provisions of this part.
