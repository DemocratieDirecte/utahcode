17B-1-217.   Conclusive presumption regarding creation and existence.
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a local district shall be
conclusively presumed to have been lawfully created and existing if for two
years following the district's creation under Subsection 17B-1-215(4):
(1)  the district has:
(a)  levied and collected a tax; or
(b)  collected a fee, charge, or assessment for a commodity, service,
facility, or improvement provided by the district; and
(2)  no challenge has been filed in court to the existence or creation of the
district.
