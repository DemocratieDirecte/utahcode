17B-2a-902.   Provisions applicable to service areas.
(1)  Each service area is governed by and has the powers stated in:
(a)  this part; and
(b)  Chapter 1, Provisions Applicable to All Local Districts.
(2)  This part applies only to service areas.
(3)  A service area is not subject to the provisions of any other part of this
chapter.
(4)  If there is a conflict between a provision in Chapter 1, Provisions
Applicable to All Local Districts, and a provision in this part, the provision
in this part governs.
