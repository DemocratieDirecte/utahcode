13-32a-102.5.   Administration and enforcement.
(1)  The division shall administer and enforce this chapter in accordance with
the authority under Title 13, Chapter 2, Division of Consumer Protection.
(2)  The attorney general, upon request, shall give legal advice to, and act
as counsel for, the division in the exercise of its responsibilities under
this chapter.
(3)  Reasonable attorney fees, costs, and interest shall be awarded to the
division in any action brought to enforce the provisions of this chapter.
