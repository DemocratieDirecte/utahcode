13-43-205.   Advisory opinion.

At any time before a final decision on a land use application by a local appeal authority under Section 10-9a-708 or 17-27a-708, a local government or a potentially aggrieved person may, in accordance with Section 13-43-206, request a written advisory opinion from a neutral third party to determine compliance with:

(1)  Sections 10-9a-507 through 10-9a-511;

(2)  Sections 17-27a-506 through 17-27a-510; and

(3)  Title 11, Chapter 36, Impact Fees Act.
