13-42-126.   Termination of agreements.
(1)  If an individual who has entered into an agreement fails for 60 days to
make payments required by the agreement, a provider may terminate the
agreement.
(2)  If a provider or an individual terminates an agreement, the provider
shall immediately return to the individual:
(a)  any money of the individual held in trust for the benefit of the
individual; and
(b)  65% of any portion of the set-up fee received pursuant to Subsection
13-42-123(4)(b) which has not been credited against settlement fees.
