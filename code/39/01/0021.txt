39-1-21.   Adjutant general -- Salary -- Bond.

The adjutant general shall receive a salary established by the governor within the salary range fixed by the Legislature in Title 67, Chapter 22, State Officer Compensation.  He shall give an official bond to the state in the penal sum of $10,000 to cover all duties imposed and offices conferred by law or authority on the adjutant general.  He shall devote all of his time during the office hours of the military department to the duties of his office.
