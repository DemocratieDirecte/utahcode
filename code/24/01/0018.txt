24-1-18.   Criminal Forfeiture Restricted Account.
(1)  There is created within the General Fund a restricted account known as
the Criminal Forfeiture Restricted Account.
(2)  Proceeds from forfeited property and forfeited money through state
forfeitures shall be deposited in this account.
(3)  Money in the account shall be appropriated to the Commission on Criminal
and Juvenile Justice for implementing the Crime Reduction Assistance Program
under Section 24-1-19.
