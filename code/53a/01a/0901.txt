53A-1a-901.    Definitions.

As used in this part:

(1)  "Extended-day kindergarten" means a kindergarten program that provides:

(a)  at least the same number of instructional hours per school year as first grade, as established under State Board of Education rule; or

(b)  additional instruction hours that are outside the regular school year, including summer programs.

(2)  "Part-day kindergarten" means a kindergarten program that provides fewer instructional hours than first grade, as established under State Board of Education rule.
