53A-1a-501.3.   Definitions.
As used in this part:
(1)  "Asset" means property of all kinds, real and personal, tangible and
intangible, and includes:
(a)  cash;
(b)  stock or other investments;
(c)  real property;
(d)  equipment and supplies;
(e)  an ownership interest;
(f)  a license;
(g)  a cause of action; and
(h)  any similar property.
(2)  "Chartering entity" means the entity that authorizes the establishment of
a charter school.
