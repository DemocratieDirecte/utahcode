53A-11a-302.   Model policy and State Board of Education duties.

On or before September 1, 2011, the State Board of Education shall:

(1)  develop a model policy on bullying, cyber-bullying, harassment, hazing, and retaliation; and

(2)  post the model policy described in Subsection (1) on the State Board of Education's website.
