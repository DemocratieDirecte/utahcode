53A-10-102.5.   Local school board's responsibilities for an educator
evaluation program.
A local school board shall:
(1)  develop an educator evaluation program in accordance with this chapter
and support, monitor, and maintain the program; and
(2)  provide for:
(a)  the evaluation of provisional and probationary educators at least twice
each school year;
(b)  the ongoing evaluation of all career educators; and
(c)  an orientation on the educator evaluation program.
