53A-9-101.   Purpose.
(1)  The Legislature recognizes the importance of rewarding educators who
strive to improve the quality of education, of providing incentives for
educators employed by the public schools to continue to pursue excellence in
education, of rewarding educators who demonstrate the achievement of
excellence, and of properly compensating educators who assume additional
educational responsibilities.
(2)  In order to achieve these goals and to provide educators with increased
opportunities for professional growth, school districts are authorized and
encouraged to develop career ladder programs.
