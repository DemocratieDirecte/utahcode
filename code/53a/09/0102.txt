53A-9-102.   Definitions.
As used in this chapter:
(1)  "Career ladder" means a compensation system developed by a school
district, with advice and counsel from parents, teachers, and school
administrators who represent the various schools throughout the district,
which is in accordance with provisions of this chapter and applicable policies
and guidelines adopted by the State Board of Education.
(2)  "Educator" or "teacher" means certified personnel who are paid on the
teacher's salary schedule and whose primary function is to provide
instructional or a combination of instructional and counseling services to
students in the public schools.
(3)  "Evaluation system" means the educator evaluation program developed under
Title 53A, Chapter 10, Educator Evaluation.
