53A-17a-114.   Career and technical education program alternatives.
(1)  If a school district determines that a secondary student's career and
technical education goals are better achieved at a regional applied technology
college created under Title 53B, Chapter 2a, Utah College of Applied
Technology, the student may attend that institution.
(2)  Students served under this section in a regional applied technology
college shall continue to be counted in the regular school program average
daily membership of the sending school district.
