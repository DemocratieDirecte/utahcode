53A-20b-105.   Limited obligations.
Bonds, notes, and other obligations issued by the authority:
(1)  do not constitute a debt, moral obligation, or liability of the state, or
of any county, city, town, school district, or any other political subdivision
of the state;
(2)  do not constitute the loan of credit of the state or of any county, city,
town, school district, or any other political subdivision of the state; and
(3)  may not be paid from funds other than loan payments or lease revenues
received from a charter school or other funds pledged by a charter school.
