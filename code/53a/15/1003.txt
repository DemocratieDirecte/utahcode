53A-15-1003.   Courses and credit.
(1)  The Electronic High School shall:
(a)  offer courses in an open-entry, open-exit format; and
(b)  offer core curriculum courses that are in conformance with course
standards and objectives established by the board.
(2)  Public schools shall:
(a)  accept all credits awarded to students by the Electronic High School; and
(b)  apply credits awarded for a core curriculum course toward the fulfillment
of core curriculum requirements.
