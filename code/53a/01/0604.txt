53A-1-604.   Test development, publication, and administration.
(1)  The State Board of Education shall develop, publish, and administer
criterion-referenced tests and a tenth grade basic skills competency test.
(2)  The board may use the expert services of any person in the public or
private sector in:
(a)  evaluating current tests and assessment programs; or
(b)  developing, publishing, and administering new tests.
