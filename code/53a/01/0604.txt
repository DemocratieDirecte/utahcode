53A-1-604.   Test development, publication, and administration.
(1)  The State Board of Education shall develop, publish, and administer
criterion-referenced tests and other assessments referred to in Subsections
53A-1-603(2)(c) and (d) of its own devising and incorporate a norm-referenced
test into U-PASS.
(2)  The board may use the expert services of any persons or groups of persons
in the public or private sector in evaluating current tests and assessment
programs, in developing, publishing, and administering new tests, or both.
