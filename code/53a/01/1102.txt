53A-1-1102.   Definitions.

As used in this part:

(1)  "High school" means a school that includes grade 12.

(2)  "Statewide assessment" means any of the following assessments that are administered statewide under Part 6, Achievement Tests:

(a)  a criterion-referenced test of student achievement in language arts, mathematics, or science, including a test administered in a computer adaptive format; or

(b)  an online writing assessment.
