10-9a-607.   Dedication of streets and other public places.
(1)  Plats, when made, acknowledged, and recorded according to the procedures
specified in this part, operate as a dedication of all streets and other
public places, and vest the fee of those parcels of land in the municipality
for the public for the uses named or intended in those plats.
(2)  The dedication established by this section does not impose liability upon
the municipality for streets and other public places that are dedicated in
this manner but are unimproved.
