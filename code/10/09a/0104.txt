10-9a-104.   Stricter requirements.

(1)  Except as provided in Subsection (2), a municipality may enact an ordinance imposing stricter requirements or higher standards than are required by this chapter.

(2)  A municipality may not impose stricter requirements or higher standards than are required by:

(a)  Section 10-9a-305;

(b)  Section 10-9a-514;

(c)  Section 10-9a-516; and

(d)  Section 10-9a-520.
