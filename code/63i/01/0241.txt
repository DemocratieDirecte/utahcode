63I-1-241.   Repeal dates, Title 41.
The following provisions of Title 41 are repealed on the following dates:
(1)  Title 41, Chapter 12a, Part 8, Uninsured Motorist Identification Database
Program, is repealed July 1, 2010.
(2)  The HOV lane exception in Subsection 41-6a-702(5) is repealed December
31, 2010.
