63I-1-278.   Repeal dates, Title 78A and Title 78B.
(1)  The Office of the Court Administrator, created in Section 78A-2-105, is
repealed July 1, 2018.
(2)  The case management program coordinator in Subsection 78A-2-108(4) is
repealed July 1, 2009.
(3)  Section 78B-3-421, regarding medical malpractice arbitration agreements,
is repealed July 1, 2019.
(4)  Alternative Dispute Resolution Act, created in Title 78B, Chapter 6, Part
2, is repealed July 1, 2016.
