61-2c-106 (Superseded 01/01/10).   Addresses provided the division.
(1) (a)  In providing an address to the division under this chapter, a
physical location or street address shall be provided.
(b)  The following are public information:
(i)  a business address; or
(ii)  a mailing address.
(2)  An individual or entity licensed under this chapter will be considered to
have received any notification that is mailed to the last mailing address
furnished to the division by the  individual, or by the principal lending
manager of the entity, licensed under this chapter.
