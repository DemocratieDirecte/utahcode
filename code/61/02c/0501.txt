61-2c-501.   Fund created -- Minimum balance.
(1) (a)  There is created a restricted special revenue fund known as the
"Residential Mortgage Loan Education, Research, and Recovery Fund."
(b)  As used in this part, "fund" means the Residential Mortgage Loan
Education, Research, and Recovery Fund.
(2)  The interest earned on the fund shall be deposited into the fund.
(3) (a)  At the beginning of each state fiscal year, $100,000 shall remain
available in the fund to satisfy judgments rendered against a person licensed
under this chapter.
(b)  For purposes of this part, a "judgment" includes a criminal restitution
judgment.
