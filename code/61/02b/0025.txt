61-2b-25.   Other law unaffected.
This chapter may not be considered to prohibit a person approved, licensed,
certified, or registered under this chapter from engaging in the practice of
real estate appraising as a professional corporation or a limited liability
company in accordance with:
(1)  Title 16, Chapter 11, Professional Corporation Act; or
(2)  Title 48, Chapter 2c, Utah Revised Limited Liability Company Act.
