63H-1-502.   Allowable uses of tax increment and other funds.
(1)  The authority may use tax increment and other funds available to the
authority, other than municipal services revenue:
(a)  for any of the purposes for which the use of tax increment is authorized
under this chapter;
(b)  for administrative, overhead, legal, and other operating expenses of the
authority;
(c)  to pay for, including financing or refinancing, all or part of the
development of military land within the project area from which the tax
increment funds were collected;
(d)  to pay the cost of the installation and construction of any publicly
owned infrastructure and improvements within the project area from which the
tax increment funds were collected;
(e)  to pay the cost of the installation of publicly owned infrastructure and
improvements outside the project area from which the tax increment funds were
collected if the authority board determines by resolution that the
infrastructure and improvements are of benefit to the project area; and
(f)  to pay the principal of and interest on bonds issued by the authority.
(2)  The determination of the authority board under Subsection (1)(e)
regarding benefit to the project area shall be final and conclusive.
