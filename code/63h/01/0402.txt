63H-1-402.   Public meeting to consider and discuss draft project area plan
-- Notice -- Adoption of plan.
(1)  The authority board shall hold at least one public meeting to consider
and discuss the draft project area plan.
(2)  At least ten days before holding a public meeting under Subsection (1),
the authority board shall give notice of the public meeting to taxing
entities.
(3)  Following consideration and discussion of the project area plan, the
board may adopt the draft project area plan as the project area plan.
