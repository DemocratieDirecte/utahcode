59-12-103.2.   Remote Sales Restricted Account -- Creation -- Funding for
account -- Interest.
(1)  There is created within the General Fund a restricted account known as
the "Remote Sales Restricted Account."
(2)  The account shall be funded from the portion of the sales and use tax
deposited by the commission as provided in Section 59-12-103.
(3) (a)  The account shall earn interest.
(b)  The interest described in Subsection (3)(a) shall be deposited into the
account.
