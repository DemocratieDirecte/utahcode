59-2-1111 (Superseded 01/01/11).   Exemption of property used for irrigation
purposes.
Water rights, ditches, canals, reservoirs, power plants, pumping plants,
transmission lines, pipes, and flumes owned and used by individuals or
corporations for irrigating land within the state owned by those individuals
or corporations, or by the individual members of the corporation, are exempt
from taxation to the extent that they are owned and used for irrigation
purposes.
