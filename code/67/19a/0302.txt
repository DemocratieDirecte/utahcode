67-19a-302.   Levels of procedure.
(1)  A career service employee may grieve the issues specified under
Subsection 67-19a-202(1)(a) to all levels of the grievance procedure described
in Section 67-19a-402.
(2) (a)  A career service employee may grieve all other matters only to the
level of the department head.
(b)  The decision of the department head on a matter under Subsection (2)(a)
is final and may not be advanced to the office.
