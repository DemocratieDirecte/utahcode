20A-4-502.   Altering vote count or returns.

(1)  It is unlawful for any person to:

(a)  willfully add to or subtract from the votes actually cast at an election in any election returns; or

(b)  alter any election returns.

(2)  Any person who violates this section may be sentenced to imprisonment in the state prison for not less than one nor more than five years.
