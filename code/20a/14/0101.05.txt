20A-14-101.5.   State Board of Education -- Number of members -- State Board of Education district boundaries.

(1)  The State Board of Education shall consist of 15 members, with one member to be elected from each State Board of Education district.

(2)  The Legislature adopts the official census population figures and maps of the Bureau of the Census of the United States Department of Commerce developed in connection with the taking of the 2010 national decennial census as the official data for establishing State Board of Education district boundaries.

(3) (a)  The Legislature enacts the numbers and boundaries of the State Board of Education districts designated in the Board block assignment file that is the electronic component of the bill that enacts this section.

(b)  That Board block assignment file, and the State Board of Education district boundaries generated from that Board block assignment file, may be accessed via the Utah Legislature's website.
