20A-14-102.1 (Superseded 01/01/12).   Omissions from maps -- How resolved.

(1)  If any area of the state is omitted from a State Board of Education district in the maps enacted by the Legislature, the county clerk of the affected county, upon discovery of the omission, shall attach the area to the appropriate state board district according to the requirements of Subsections (2) and (3).

(2)  If the area is surrounded by a state board district, the area shall be attached to that district.

(3)  If the area is contiguous to two districts, the area shall be attached to the district that has the least population.

(4)  Any attachment made under Subsection (1) shall be certified in writing and filed with the lieutenant governor.
