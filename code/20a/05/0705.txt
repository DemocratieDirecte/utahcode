20A-5-705.   Officer or watcher revealing vote.
(1)  It is unlawful for any election official or watcher to reveal to any
other person the name of any candidate for whom a voter has voted, or to
communicate to another his opinion, belief, or impression as to how or for
whom a voter has voted.
(2)  Any election official or watcher who violates this section is guilty of a
class A misdemeanor and shall be imprisoned for not less than six months, and
fined not less than $250.
