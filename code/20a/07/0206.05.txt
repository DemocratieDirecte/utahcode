20A-7-206.5.   Financial disclosure -- Paid circulators.
(1)  When the proponents of a proposed initiative have paid persons to
circulate the petition, the proponents shall, at the time the last initiative
packet is filed with the county clerk, file a form with the lieutenant
governor detailing the amount of money paid per signature.
(2)  The lieutenant governor shall develop a form to disclose the information
required by this section.
