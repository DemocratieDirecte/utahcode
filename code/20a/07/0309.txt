20A-7-309.   Form of ballot -- Manner of voting.
(1)  The county clerks shall ensure that the number and ballot title verified
to them by the lieutenant governor are presented upon the official ballot
with, immediately adjacent to them, the words "For" and "Against," each word
presented with an adjacent square in which the elector may indicate his vote.
(2)  Voters desiring to vote in favor of preventing the law that is the
subject of the referendum from taking effect shall mark the square adjacent to
the word "For," and those desiring to vote against preventing the law that is
the subject of the referendum petition from taking effect shall mark the
square adjacent to the word "Against."
