20A-1-202.   Date and purpose of local elections.
(1)  A municipal general election shall be held in municipalities and local
districts on the first Tuesday after the first Monday in November of each odd-
numbered year.
(2)  At the municipal general election, the voters shall:
(a) (i)  choose persons to serve as municipal officers; and
(ii)  choose persons to serve as local district officers; and
(b)  approve or reject:
(i)  any proposed initiatives or referenda that have qualified for the ballot
as provided by law; and
(ii)  any other ballot propositions submitted to the voters that are
authorized by the Utah Code.
