20A-3-404.5 (Repealed 01/01/12).   Special overseas citizen voter absentee ballot.

(1)  As used in this section, "federal office" means President/Vice President of the United States, United States Senator, and United States Representative.

(2)  Each county clerk, after consulting with the chief election officer, shall prepare a special overseas citizen voter ballot containing a means for the overseas citizen voter to vote for each federal office that will appear on the regular general election ballot.
