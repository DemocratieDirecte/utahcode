9-4-621.   Issuance of bonds -- Other laws not to apply.
This part constitutes full authority for the authorization and issuance of
bonds.  No other law with regard to the authorization or issuance of
obligations or the deposit of their proceeds that requires a bond election or
in any way impedes or restricts the carrying out of the acts authorized to be
done shall be construed as applying to any proceedings taken or acts done
pursuant to this part.
