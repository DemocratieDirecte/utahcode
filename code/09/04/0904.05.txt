9-4-904.5.   Corporation as continuation of agency.
(1)  Beginning July 1, 2001, the Utah Housing Finance Agency shall become
known as the Utah Housing Corporation.
(2)  The corporation is a continuation of the Utah Housing Finance Agency and
shall:
(a)  possess all rights, title, privileges, powers, immunities, property, and
claims of the agency; and
(b)  fulfill and perform all obligations of the agency, including all agency
obligations relating to outstanding bonds and notes.
