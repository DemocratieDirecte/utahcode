9-4-1408.    Recognition of community action agencies.
The office shall have the power to:
(1)  recognize eligible organizations as community action agencies;
(2)  withdraw the recognition or terminate funding of a designated community
action agency for cause, as established by rule; or
(3)  change the boundaries and the number of recognized community action
agencies, provided that the governing board of each affected community action
agency concurs in the action.
