9-12-102.   Assistance to low-income persons -- Contracts -- Administration.
(1)  The department may assist certain low-income families and individuals in
the payment of home energy costs.  Assistance given shall be made available to
households throughout the state, irrespective of the source of household
energy supply.
(2)  The department may contract with one or more public or private agencies
to distribute and administer these funds subject to the criteria established
by the department.
