77-24-5.   Property seized from person -- Duplicate receipts.
(1)  When tangible or intangible property is seized with or without a warrant,
the peace officer seizing it shall at the time deliver a receipt to the person
from whom it is taken and file a duplicate in the office of the agency
employing the officer.
(2)  If the property is seized for forfeiture, a written notice pursuant to
Section 24-1-4 may serve as the receipt, and a duplicate filed in the office
of the agency employing the officer.
(3)  If custody of the property is transferred to another police agency or the
property is placed in evidence, a copy of the receipt shall accompany it until
disposition of the property is made in accordance with applicable law.
