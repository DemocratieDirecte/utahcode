41-6a-802.   Turning around -- Where prohibited -- Visibility.
The operator of a vehicle may not make a U-turn or turn the vehicle to proceed
in the opposite direction:
(1)  unless the movement can be made safely and without interfering with other
traffic; or
(2)  on any curve, or upon the approach to, or near the crest of a grade, if
the vehicle is not visible at a distance of 500 feet by the operator of any
other vehicle approaching from either direction.
