41-6a-401.5.   Accident involving death -- Stop at accident -- Penalty.

(1)  The operator of a vehicle involved in an accident resulting in the death of a person shall:

(a)  immediately stop the vehicle at the scene of the accident or as close to it as possible without obstructing traffic more than is necessary; and

(b)  remain at the scene of the accident until the operator has fulfilled the requirements of Section 41-6a-401.7.

(2) A person who violates the provisions of Subsection (1) is guilty of a third degree felony and shall be fined not less than $750.
