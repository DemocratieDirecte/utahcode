41-1a-601.   Lien validity -- Security interest.
(1)  Except as provided under Subsection (2), a lien upon a vehicle, vessel,
or outboard motor, except a lien dependent upon possession, is not valid
against the creditors of an owner acquiring a lien by levy or attachment, or
subsequent purchasers, or encumbrancers without notice until Sections
41-1a-602 through 41-1a-606 have been complied with.
(2)  Security interests in inventory consisting in part of vehicles subject to
registration under this chapter, that are held for sale by a person in the
business of selling goods of that kind, shall be perfected under Section 70A-
9a-310, except that buyers in the ordinary course of business, as defined in
Section 70A-1a-201, take free of the security interests as provided in Section
70A-9a-320.
