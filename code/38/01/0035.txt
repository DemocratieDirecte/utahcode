38-1-35.   Limitation of liability.
(1)  The state and the state's agencies, instrumentalities, political
subdivisions, and an employee of a governmental entity are immune from suit
for any injury resulting from the state construction registry.
(2)  The designated agent and its principals, agents, and employees are not
liable to any person for the accuracy, coherence, suitability, completeness,
or legal effectiveness of information filed or searched in the database if the
designated agent:
(a)  develops and maintains the database in compliance with reliability,
availability, and security standards established by the division; and
(b)  meets data entry accuracy standards established by the division under
Subsection 38-1-30(6)(b).
(3)  The designated agent and its principals, agents, and employees are not
liable for their inability to perform obligations under this chapter to the
extent performance of those obligations is prevented by:
(a)  an act of God;
(b)  a fire;
(c)  a storm;
(d)  an earthquake;
(e)  an accident;
(f)  governmental interference; or
(g)  any other event or cause beyond the designated agent's control.
