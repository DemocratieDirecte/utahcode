38-1-17.   Costs -- Apportionment -- Costs and attorneys' fee to
subcontractor.
Except as provided in Section 38-11-107, as between the owner and the
contractor the court shall apportion the costs according to the right of the
case, but in all cases each subcontractor exhibiting a lien shall have his
costs awarded to him, including the costs of preparing and recording the
notice of claim of lien and such reasonable attorneys' fee as may be incurred
in preparing and recording said notice of claim of lien.
