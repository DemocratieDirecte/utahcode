76-10-1107.   Seizure and sale of devices or equipment used for gambling.
When any magistrate determines that any devices or equipment are being used or
kept for the purpose of being used for gambling, the magistrate may authorize
the county commissioner of the county where the seizure occurred, in
conjunction with the sheriff, or if the seizure occurred within the limits of
an incorporated city or town, may authorize its governing body, in conjunction
with its chief law enforcement officer, to seize the devices or equipment and
institute forfeiture proceedings in accordance with the procedures and
substantive protections of Title 24, Chapter 1, Utah Uniform Forfeiture
Procedures Act.
