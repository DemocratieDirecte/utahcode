76-7-314.5.   Killing an unborn child.
(1)  A person is guilty of killing an unborn child if the person causes the
death of an unborn child by performing an abortion of the unborn child in
violation of the provisions of Subsection 76-7-302(3).
(2)  Notwithstanding any other provision of this section, a woman who seeks to
have, or obtains, an abortion for herself is not criminally liable.
