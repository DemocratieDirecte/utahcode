53B-8a-111.   Annual audit of financial statements -- Information to
governor and Legislature.
(1)  The financial statements of the Utah Educational Savings Plan Trust shall
be audited annually by the state auditor or the state auditor's designee and
reported in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles.
(2)  The board shall submit to the governor and the Legislature:
(a)  upon request, any studies or evaluations of the Utah Educational Savings
Plan Trust;
(b)  upon request, a summary of the benefits provided by the Utah Educational
Savings Plan Trust including the number of participants and beneficiaries in
the Utah Educational Savings Plan Trust; and
(c)  upon request, any other information which is relevant in order to make a
full, fair, and effective disclosure of the operations of the Utah Educational
Savings Plan Trust.
