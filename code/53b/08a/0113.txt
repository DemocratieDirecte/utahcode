53B-8a-113.   Property rights to assets in Utah Educational Savings Plan
Trust.
(1)  The assets of the Utah Educational Savings Plan Trust, including the
program fund and the endowment fund, shall at all times be preserved,
invested, and expended solely and only for the purposes of the Utah
Educational Savings Plan Trust and shall be held in trust for the account
owners and beneficiaries.
(2)  No property rights in the Utah Educational Savings Plan Trust shall exist
in favor of the state.
(3)  The assets may not be transferred or used by the state for any purposes
other than the purposes of the Utah Educational Savings Plan Trust.
