6-1-15.   Debts not matured -- Delay in filing claims.
Any creditor may claim debts to become due as well as debts due, but on debts
not due a reasonable rebate shall be made when the same are not drawing
interest. Creditors who shall not file their claims within three months from
the publication of notice as aforesaid shall not participate in dividends
until after payment in full of all claims presented within said time and
allowed by the court, unless the court has extended the time for filing such
claims.
