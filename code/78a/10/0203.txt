78A-10-203.   Procedure.
(1)  Except for the chief justice of the Supreme Court, each commissioner is a
voting member of the Appellate Court Nominating Commission.
(2)  Four commissioners are a quorum.
(3)  The state court administrator shall serve as secretary to the Appellate
Court Nominating Commission.
(4)  The chief justice of the Supreme Court shall:
(a)  ensure that the commission follows the rules promulgated by the Judicial
Council; and
(b)  resolve any questions regarding those rules.
(5)  A member of the commission who is also a member of the Utah State Bar may
recuse himself if there is a conflict of interest that makes the member unable
to serve.
