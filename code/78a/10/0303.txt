78A-10-303.   Procedure.
(1)  Except for the chief justice of the Supreme Court, each trial court
nominating commissioner is a voting member of the commission.
(2)  Four commissioners are a quorum.
(3)  The state court administrator shall serve as secretary to each Trial
Court Nominating Commission.
(4)  The chief justice of the Supreme Court shall:
(a)  ensure that each Trial Court Nominating Commission follows the rules
promulgated by the Judicial Council; and
(b)  resolve any questions regarding those rules.
(5)  A member of a Trial Court Nominating Commission who is also a member of
the Utah State Bar may recuse himself if there is a conflict of interest that
makes the member unable to serve.
