73-2-2.   Oath and bond.
Before entering upon the duties of his office the state engineer shall take
and subscribe the constitutional oath of office. He shall file the oath with
the Division of Archives and his official bond with the state treasurer in the
penal sum of $5,000, with not less than two sureties, to be approved by the
Division of Finance, conditioned for the faithful discharge of the duties of
his office and for the delivery to his successor, or other officer appointed
by the governor to receive the same, of all moneys, books and other property
belonging to the state then in his hands or under his control, or with which
he may be legally chargeable as such officer.
